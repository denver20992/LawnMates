# Post-MVP Epic Plan – LawnMates

This document outlines proposed epics for post-MVP enhancements to expand user engagement, operational automation, and platform growth.

---

## Epic 7: User Reviews & Ratings

**Goal:** Enable transparent feedback via reviews to build trust and improve job quality.

* Story 7.1: Leave review after job completion (both roles)
* Story 7.2: Display rating average on user profile
* Story 7.3: Admin moderation for flagged reviews

## Epic 8: Repeat Booking & Favorites

**Goal:** Make it easier for users to rebook trusted landscapers or repost similar jobs.

* Story 8.1: “Rebook Job” from history or chat
* Story 8.2: Save landscaper/property as favorite
* Story 8.3: Suggested repeat intervals and reminders

## Epic 9: Subscription & Loyalty System

**Goal:** Drive repeat use and platform loyalty via incentives.

* Story 9.1: Monthly plan for recurring lawn care
* Story 9.2: Points or credits for frequent users
* Story 9.3: Referral bonuses and promo codes

## Epic 10: Mobile App Shell (PWA / Native)

**Goal:** Enhance mobile experience with a dedicated app version.

* Story 10.1: Progressive Web App (PWA) install prompt
* Story 10.2: Push notification support
* Story 10.3: Native wrappers for iOS/Android via Capacitor or Expo

## Epic 11: Advanced Admin & Analytics

**Goal:** Expand admin control and insights.

* Story 11.1: Geographic/job volume heatmaps
* Story 11.2: Anomaly detection (e.g. high dispute rate)
* Story 11.3: Admin roles & permissions

## Epic 12: AI-Enhanced Support & Automation

**Goal:** Use AI to streamline moderation and support.

* Story 12.1: Auto-classify job photos for trust scoring
* Story 12.2: AI assistant for basic support and help
* Story 12.3: Auto-flag pattern detection in job or message history
